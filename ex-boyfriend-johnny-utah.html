15. Who is Doja Cat's ex-boyfriend Johnny Utah?

Doja Cat dated musician Johnny Utah from 2019 until she announced their split in February 2020. "Everything is okay. I unfollowed my ex-boyfriend and everything is okay. We have been cool," she said during an Instagram Live at the time. "Nothing weird going on. No drama, or weird sh*t going on. Sh*t just didn't work out." (Credit: Instagram)
