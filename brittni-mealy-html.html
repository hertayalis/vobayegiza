Brittni Mealy - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-08-30

Entrepreneur and mother to one of the rapper Future's children. She became the CEO and founder of the clothing company Pop Of Junk based in Atlanta. In 2015 she also announced the creation of a fitness community website called Babe Camp and a 10 minute makeover series through Pop Of Junk.

Before Fame

She gained prominence by attacking or defending herself against Future's groupies, baby mamas, and others on Instagram.

Trivia

She was featured on Upscale Magazine's Mommy and Mogul website in July 2015.

Family Life

Her son's name is Prince.

Associated With

She had a child with rapper Future, who only gained prominence because he was engaged to singer Ciara.
