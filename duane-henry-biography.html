Who is Duane Henry?

Duane Henry is a British actor and model. He is popular for his acting skills in The Dark Knight Rises (2012), Captain Marvel (2019), and The Oxford Murders (2008).

From 2016 till 2018, he played the character of Clayton Reeves in NCIS: Naval Criminal Investigative Service (TV Series).

Who are the parents of Duane Henry?

Duane Henry’s birthdate is 18 March 1985 and he was born in Birmingham, West Midlands, England. The details of his mother along with his father are not disclosed.

But he was raised by his single mom and she gave birth to him at the age of 16.

Duane’s ancestry is All-English and regarding his academics, Duane Henry attended St. Francis Catholic Primary School in Birmingham. 

Then he went to St. John Wall School in Handsworth, later, he enrolled at Dudley College to study drama.
