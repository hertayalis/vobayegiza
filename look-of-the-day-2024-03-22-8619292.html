Kylie Jenner Paired a Black Latex Dress With Cinderella Slippers

Publish date: 2024-09-05

There’s a lot of uncertainty in this world, but one thing is for sure: The KarJenners will bring the style heat. Whether you vibe with their braless fashion moments or prefer sleek separates, they make a statement, and we take notice — and Kylie Jenner stuck to the family’s show-stopping theme for a recent outing

Last night, Jenner attended the West Hollywood launch party of her new vodka soda drink, Sprinter. Jenner wore a skin-tight, mid-length elastic dress with a plunging neckline that revealed some side cleavage. Aside from a tiny chain bracelet and, of course, a can of Sprinter, the only other fashion item included were her Cinderella-like heels — and even those were barely noticeable.   

Jeffrey Campbell Millenni Pointed-Toe Kitten Heel

BCBGeneration Martina Sandal

I thought the reality star was barefoot, making me do a double-take and squinting to see the naked shoes that were actually on her feet. The thin, transparent upper made them practically invisible, while a round toe box and pencil-thin heel will have you pulling out your magnifying glass. I’d go so far as to call them foot gloves, and they’re a direct response to the minimalist trend so many have gravitated toward.  

Jennifer Lopez and Amal Clooney are proof of this favored style, relying on the sexy shoe for countless occasions. Jennifer Aniston and Hailey Bieber are also on board, while I have similar footwear stashed in my closet — and it’s no coincidence that this vanishing look has garnered so much love. 

While adding naked heels to your collection may seem daunting, they’re actually quite practical. The versatile footwear pairs with your entire wardrobe — and I mean everything — including baby tees, ribbed boyfriend jeans, plaid skorts, tiered maxi dresses, and sheath gowns. This is because the shoes are literally clear, which means you don’t have to worry about matching colors or coordinating prints. It takes fashion theorizing out of the equation and gives you a solid answer to all of your style needs.  

You also don’t have to opt for Jenner’s exact take on PVC heels. Consider these Jeffrey Campbell kitten heels for a pointed-toe take on the look, or these open-toed BCBGeneration sandals for something more breathable. As long as they’re see-through, you’re doing it right.
