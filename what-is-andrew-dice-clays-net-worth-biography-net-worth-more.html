Who is Andrew Dice Clay?

Andrew Dice Clay whose birth name is Andrew Clay Silverstein was born on September 29, 1957, in Brooklyn, New York in the United States of America. He grew up in a Jewish household with his mother Jacqueline, his father Fred, a real estate agent and boxer, and a sister. Andrew began doing impressions for his family at age 5 and playing drums at 7. As a teenager, he studied at James Madison High School and played drums at weddings and bar mitzvahs in the Catskills. After high school, Andrew attended Kingsborough Community College but dropped out to focus on stand-up comedy.
