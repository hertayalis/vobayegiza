Adam Joseph Scherr, a former strongman who now competes as a professional wrestler under the ring name Braun Strowman, is an American. He was born on September 6, 1983, and as of right now, he is a WWE performer for the Raw brand. Along with being a fierce solo opponent, Strowman and Ricochet make a powerful tag team.

Strowman has received notable honors from WWE over the course of his career. With partners Nicholas and Seth Rollins, he has a stellar resume that includes reigns as the Universal Champion, Intercontinental Champion, and two-time Raw Tag Team Champion. He also won the Greatest Royal Rumble title contest, the Men's Money in the Bank contract match, and the 2019 André the Giant Memorial Battle Royal, making him the only wrestler to win both a Rumble and an André Battle Royal.

For the WWE, Strowman has been the main attraction at a number of pay-per-view events, proving his status as a star draw. He has left his mark on history by holding the records for the most eliminations in a single André Battle Royal (14), the most eliminations in a single Royal Rumble match (13), and the most eliminations in an Elimination Chamber match (5, tied with Shayna Baszler). He also holds the record for having four eliminations in a single Survivor Series elimination match.

When he made his main roster debut in 2015, Strowman quickly earned a reputation as a member of The Wyatt Family's evil horde by donning a black sheep mask and establishing his authority through raw strength. Strowman has been presented as an unstoppable force during his time in the WWE, earning him the nickname "The Monster Among Men." His reappearance in 2022 only served to reinforce his status as "The Monster of all Monsters," underscoring his unrivaled presence in the squared circle.
