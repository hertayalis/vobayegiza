Early Life and Career Beginnings

Alli Webb was born and raised in South Florida. She began her career in the fashion industry, working as a public relations and marketing executive. However, her passion for hairstyling led her to attend the Art Institute of Fort Lauderdale, where she earned a degree in fashion merchandising.

Transition to the Beauty Industry

Webb’s transition to the beauty industry began when she moved to New York City to train with Toni & Guy. She then worked as a professional hairstylist for several years before moving to California with her husband, where she started a mobile blowout service in 2008.

Founding of Drybar

In 2010, Webb opened the first Drybar salon in Brentwood, California. The concept was simple: “No cuts. No color. Just blowouts.” This unique approach quickly gained popularity, and Drybar expanded rapidly across the United States.

By 2024, Drybar has over 100 locations across the U.S. and Canada. The company also launched its line of hair styling products and tools, which are sold in their salons and major retailers like Sephora and Nordstrom.
