Katie Holmes Kisses Emilio Vitolo in New York While Wearing Denim

Publish date: 2024-08-03

Katie Holmes is continuing her streak of New York City PDA with her new beau, chef Emilio Vitolo.

The happy couple was caught kissing on the sidewalk before Holmes hopped her car. Both Holmes and Vitolo looked seriously smitten, and they couldn't stop smiling throughout the kiss, either.

For the occasion, Holmes wore a black and white gingham jacket with baggy jeans and sneakers. The actress and her beau also wore masks, apart from when they were kissing.

T.JACKSON / BACKGRID

A source recently revealed to Entertainment Tonight that Holmes wouldn't be able to take Vitolo home to meet her family in Ohio due to the ongoing pandemic restrictions. However, Holmes is reportedly already close to Vitolo's brother and father, with the source saying, "Katie gets along great with Emilio's family. She fits right in and is totally comfortable around them."

"It's pretty tricky for Emilio to do any traveling right now because of the new restrictions in place," the source told ET. "Katie is very close with her siblings and parents ... Her family would love him, he's hard-working, a big family man, and he makes her so happy."

The source continued, "Katie wasn't working or traveling as much as usual and things were slower at the restaurant for Emilio, so it really gave them the opportunity to focus on each other. Instead of a few dates over an extended period of time, they were able to have a month's worth of dates in a matter of days."
