Kate Middleton Stepped Out in the Perfect Summer Dress

Publish date: 2024-08-05

Spring has officially arrived, but Kate Middleton is already dressing for summer.

On Sunday afternoon, the Duchess of Cambridge and her husband Prince William capped off the first full day of their Caribbean tour at a family-run cocoa farm in Belize, and she wore the perfect warm weather outfit for the visit. Wearing a breezy, bright blue floral midi dress with a smocked bodice and tiered skirt by Tory Burch, Kate paired the tropical-ready garment with coordinating blue pom-pom hoop earrings, a pair of white espadrilles, and at one point, aviator sunglasses.

Rather than her typical bouncy blowout, Kate wore her hair in relaxed waves that matched the laid-back nature of the island.
