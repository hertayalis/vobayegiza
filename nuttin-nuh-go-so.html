Overall Meaning

The lyrics of Notch’s song Nuttin Nuh Go So emphasize the importance of maintaining ethical and moral standards. The song is divided into four sections, each stressing a significant aspect of living righteously. The first section is about faithfulness in relationships, indicating that one should not cheat on their partner or engage in sexual activity with anyone during their menstruation period. The second section deals with sexual behavior, highlighting the importance of intimacy between a man and a woman and condemning homosexuality. The third section discusses crime and loyalty, emphasizing that a true friend would never betray another to save themselves or join a gang to seek protection. The final section centers on drug use, endorsing the use of marijuana for meditation purposes and rejecting the use of hard drugs like cocaine.
